# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 30, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes what data Masqueroll collects, how it is used, and how you can manage or delete it.

## 2. Data Collected

Masqueroll does not maintain its own database. All data is stored as messages in a dedicated `#character-sheets` channel within your Discord server. This includes:

- **Character sheets**: name, stats, and any image you provide via the `/character` command.
- **Macros**: names and formulas you create via the `/macro` command.

The bot reads and edits those messages to provide its functionality. No data is stored outside of Discord.

## 3. How Data Is Used

Data is used exclusively to provide the bot's functionality (e.g., recalling your character stats for rolls). It is not sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data lives in your server's `#character-sheets` channel and is retained until deleted. You can remove it at any time:

- `/delete_character` — deletes the character sheet message (or forum post).
- `/remove_macro` — removes a macro line from your sheet.

Server administrators can also delete the messages or channel directly.

## 5. Third Parties

Because data is stored in Discord, it is subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy). Masqueroll itself does not share data with any third parties and uses no analytics or tracking services.

## 6. Children's Privacy

This bot is not directed at children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, do not use this bot. This aligns with Discord's own minimum age requirement.

## 7. Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 8. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding your data, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
